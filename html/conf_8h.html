int ns_startup_config_load ( sr_node_t *  node_iter)

Loads whole nemea supervisor config tree.

Load whole nemea supervisor config tree into supervisor structures (module_group_t and instance_t) and pushes them into appropriate linked lists. Parses nemea-supervisor container in YANG schema.

Parameters
node_iterFirst node obtained from root level of nemea supervisor sysrepo configugration from sr_get_subtree function.
